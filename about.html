About Me

Hello! I'm Anagha, a curious learner who loves understanding how humans think, learn, and make decisions. My journey blends computer science, psychology, and neuroscience.

I'm especially interested in how computational models can mimic cognitive processes like memory, attention, and perception. My goal is to contribute to ethical and human-aligned AI by drawing on insights from both brains and machines.

Outside of academics, you'll find me reading essays, obsessing over typography, and being passionately curious about everything from evolutionary psychology to internet subcultures.
